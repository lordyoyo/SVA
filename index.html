Welcome to the Stardew Valley Animal Profit Calculator! This tool helps you estimate your daily and seasonal earnings from your farm animals.

The calculations assume an average daily luck of 0 and maximum friendship and happiness with all animals.

Sections:

Results Table on top (empty by default):

  • Animal: The animal producing the product.
  • Product: The name of the raw or processed product.
  • Profit (Total for product): Your estimated total profit from that animal's products over the selected seasons.
  • Profit (Per Day): Your estimated average daily profit from that animal's products.

Animal Inputs:

  • Number of Animals: Input how many of each animal you own, after the name of the animal.
  • Golden Animal Crackers You can enter the amount of animals that have been given a cracker, doubling their output.
  • "Craft?" Checkbox: If checked, the calculator assumes you will process all applicable products from that animal into artisan goods. If unchecked, the products will be sold raw.

Players & Professions:

  • Players: Select 1 to 8 players to adjust profession rules.
  • Artisan: +40% selling price for artisan goods (e.g., Mayonnaise, Cheese).
  • Rancher: +20% selling price for animal products (e.g., Eggs, Milk, Wool, but NOT Truffles). Also raises most artisan good prices.
  • Coopmaster: Increases quality chances for coop animals (Eggs, Duck Feathers, Rabbit's Foot, Dinosaur Eggs, etc.).
  • Shepherd: Sheep produce wool faster and increases quality chances for sheep.
  • Gatherer: Chance for double harvest of foraged items (Truffles).
  • Botanist: Foraged items (Truffles) are always iridium quality.
  • Smart: (Currently not implemented, probably will add Treasure Hunter Manual here).

Seasons:

Check the seasons you plan to have active for animal production. Note that pigs only find truffles on non-rainy Spring, Summer, and Fall days.
